# Product Requirements Document (PRD) -- Audiofast Website

## 1. Overview

Audiofast is a Polish distributor of **high‑end audio equipment** with
nearly 20 years of experience. The company focuses on serving discerning
**audiophiles** who demand uncompromising sound fidelity. Audiofast
represents and distributes premium global brands, offering products that
cover the full spectrum of the high‑end listening experience.

## 2. Purpose

The new website should communicate Audiofast's position as a leading
distributor of high‑end audio equipment in Poland. It must highlight: -
Expertise in the audiophile market. - Exclusive representation of
premium brands. - Focus on detail and emotional connection through
music.

## 3. Scope

The project includes: - A modern website showcasing the company's
portfolio and brands. - Informational sections: About, Products, Brands,
News, Contact. - Visual emphasis on luxury and high‑end aesthetics.

## 4. Target Users

- **Audiophiles**: Users passionate about sound quality and high‑end
  listening experiences.
- **Retail Partners / Dealers**: Shops or individuals interested in
  carrying premium products.
- **Industry Professionals**: Reviewers, event organizers, and
  collaborators in the audio space.

## 5. Key Features

- **Product Catalog**: Detailed descriptions of CD players, DACs,
  amplifiers, cables, speakers, signal conditioners, and more.
- **Brand Pages**: Dedicated subpages for each represented brand
  (e.g., Wilson Audio, Synergistic Research, MUTEC).
- **News / Events Section**: Updates on exhibitions, new product
  launches, and industry news.
- **Contact Information**: Clear and accessible contact details for
  distribution inquiries.
- **High‑end Design**: Premium look and feel with strong visuals to
  reflect the luxury audio market.

## 6. Unique Value Proposition

- Exclusive distributor in Poland for selected high‑end brands.
- Trusted market presence with nearly two decades of experience.
- Commitment to perfecting every element of the listening chain ---
  from electronics to cables and accessories.
